About Us

Our Story

The Green Slice began with a simple idea: what if pizza could be indulgent and good for the planet? Born from a love of bold flavors and green living, we set out to create a place where vegetarian pizza wasn’t just an option—it was the star. From our very first slice, we’ve focused on crafting delicious food using only the freshest local ingredients, served up in a warm, welcoming space that feels like home.

Our Philosophy

We believe that good food can and should do good. That’s why every pizza at The Green Slice is made with sustainability in mind. We source ingredients from nearby farms, minimize waste in our kitchen, and opt for eco-friendly packaging—because small choices make a big impact. Whether you’re a long-time veggie lover or just exploring greener options, we’re here to serve pizza that’s better for you and the planet.
